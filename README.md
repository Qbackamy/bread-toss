# 🥖 Bread Toss 🫓 — Office Discus Championship

A browser-based, pass-the-laptop party game for 4 coworkers. Take turns hurling
a **baguette** (fast, straight, punishes bad angles) or a **naan** (floaty,
chaotic, occasionally glorious) down a discus field to see which person — and
which bread — flies furthest.

## The athletes

- **Luca** — tall, skinny, devoted to Richard Clarkson
- **Nick** — elbow ventilation for maximum aerodynamics
- **Massimo** — business up top, track & field down below
- **Doug** — the new guy; comes with his own country music sting

## How to play

1. Open `index.html` in a browser (no build, no backend, one file).
2. Pick your thrower, pick your bread.
3. Classic golf-game mechanic: click/tap/spacebar to lock the **angle**, then
   the **power**. Overfill into the red and you get a WILD throw.
4. Mind the wind — it hits the naan much harder than the baguette.
5. After all 4 throws: leaderboard, **Furthest Human** and **Superior Bread**
   awards, and a Play Again button.

Works with mouse, keyboard (spacebar), and touch.
